# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Thai PC gamers who want to experience story-driven or complex PC games in their native language without language barriers, valuing high-quality translation, easy mod installation, and clear project tracking.

## Product Purpose
To showcase high-quality, 100% free Thai game translation mods, track the current translation progress dynamically, and provide straightforward installation guides so everyone can enjoy their favorite games.

## Brand Personality
Dreamy, Dedicated, Premium (ชวนฝัน/อวกาศ, ประณีต/มุ่งมั่น, เรียบหรู/มีระดับ)

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS-monoculture templates (flat white backgrounds, boring lists, generic gray fonts, overused Inter font with extremely tight letter spacing, and nested visual cards).

## Design Principles
- **Dreamy Immersion (ชวนฝันชวนติดตาม)**: Maintain a deep space/cosmic theme using subtle stars, glowing accents, and high-contrast dark gradients that reflect the feeling of entering a dream.
- **Dedicated Clarity (ประณีตและชัดเจน)**: Ensure progress stats, installation instructions, and member credits are laid out with precision, high typography contrast, and no visual clutter.
- **Premium Simplicity (ความเรียบหรูที่เข้าถึงง่าย)**: Use smooth animations and glassmorphic elements with caution to keep the site feeling high-end but lightweight, prioritizing quick downloads.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio of text must satisfy WCAG AA standards (≥4.5:1 ratio).
- Respect system reduced-motion settings by disabling heavy animation loops when requested.
- Maintain simple keyboard navigation and highly readable Thai typography (using Sarabun and Noto Sans Thai).
